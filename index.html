Who We Are

A Home for the Muslim Community in Winnipeg

Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is a vibrant and inclusive Islamic center dedicated to serving the spiritual, educational, and social needs of Muslims in Winnipeg's Kildonan area.

We are committed to the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah, striving to promote compassion and unity among our members and the broader community.

Programs for Every Soul

From daily prayers to educational programs for all ages, Rahma Islamic Centre provides a comprehensive range of services to support your spiritual journey and community connection.

Sisters' Halaqa

A special Quran class tailored for women to study the Quran, discuss its meanings, and share spiritual insights in a welcoming, supportive environment.

Daily Five Prayers

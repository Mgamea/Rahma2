Why Donate

Be Part of Something Greater

At Rahma Islamic Centre, we are dedicated to serving our community by providing a space for worship, education, and social services. Our centre is more than just a place of prayer — it is a hub for learning, a refuge for those in need, and a beacon of unity.

Your generosity, no matter the size, will make a lasting difference for Muslims in Winnipeg.

  • Expand facilities to accommodate more worshippers
  • Offer more educational programs for children and adults
  • Support families in need through charity initiatives
  • Foster interfaith dialogue and community-building events

Who We Are

A Home for the Muslim Community

The Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre is a vibrant and inclusive Islamic Center dedicated to serving the spiritual, educational, and social needs of Muslims in Winnipeg. Our centre is a welcoming space for individuals of all ages, background, or level of religious knowledge.

Our mosque is committed to the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). We strive to promote compassion and unity among our members and the broader community.

With a focus on both religious education and community development, we offer various services to enrich the lives of our members and foster a spirit of brotherhood and sisterhood.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

At the Winnipeg Rahma Islamic Centre, we are guided by the following core values that shape everything we do.

Faith

We prioritize nurturing a deep connection with Allah (SWT) through worship, prayer, and reflection on the teachings of Islam.

Community

We believe in fostering a sense of brotherhood and sisterhood, where all members feel welcome, supported, and included.

Education

We are committed to lifelong learning, offering programs that enhance understanding of the Quran, Hadith, and Islamic teachings.

Service

We believe in giving back through charitable acts, support for the needy, and initiatives that benefit the broader society.

Integrity

We strive to uphold the highest moral and ethical standards, reflecting the principles of honesty, fairness, and respect.

Compassion

We encourage kindness and care for others, following the example of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

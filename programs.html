Programs & Services

Spiritual Counseling

Free, appointment-based counseling for youth, adults, couples, and seniors. Referrals provided where needed.

Food Bank

Supporting families in need with essential food supplies and groceries through our community food bank program.

Da'in & Dialogue

Engaging in meaningful interfaith dialogue and community outreach to build bridges and understanding.

Marriage (Nikah)

Islamic marriage contracts in compliance with Manitoba and Canadian law.

Funeral Support

Dignified, affordable funeral arrangements aligned with Islamic teachings and community support.

Religious Workshops

Practical sessions on faith and contemporary issues relevant to modern Muslim life.

Hajj & 'Umrah Guidance

Comprehensive workshops and accurate, up-to-date resources (online & at the Centre).
